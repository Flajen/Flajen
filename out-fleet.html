Our Fleet

The flagship of our company is the multi-purpose vessel Blue Sky, capable of solving many tasks related to work on the shelf. The successful design and technical equipment of the vessel allow it to be quickly re-profiled in any work from the transportation of heavy lift cargo to seabed preparations and reinstatement works, landfalls or rock installations and even cable laying.

MULTIFUNCTIONAL VESSEL «BLUE SKY»

Rock material and cryshed stone has been used for ports and coastal protection purposes for centuries — for dikes and breakwaters, groins and scour protection. Yet in the past several decades the uses of rock have taken on new dimensions in the execution of maritime infrastructure construction.

Previously referred to as rock dumping, today the term Subsea Rock Installation is more common, reflecting the advanced techniques that are being applied, in particular for the offshore oil and gas sector.

Our cable-laying vessel is a truly multipurpose. She capable of cable-laying as well as executing a wide range of associated activities such as diving support, subsea rock installation, offshore construction support, salvage assignments and even dredging operations.
