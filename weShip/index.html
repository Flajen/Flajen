QUALITY & SAFETY

Quality occupational Health & Safety environmental protection security

Quality occupational Health & Safety environmental protection security

The management of WeShip JSC are committed to ensuring healthy, injury-free workplaces and minimizing their impact on the environment. All of our staff are committed both to ensuring their own safety and to protecting the environment.

Implementation of a quality management system

Implementation of a quality management system

By implementing an integrated management system, WeShip JSC has demonstrated its commitment to ensuring its employees’ safety and protecting the environment, in addition to providing high-quality heavy-lift transport. To ensure a consistently high level of quality WeShip JSC aligns its business processes to tap synergies whilst ensuring and fostering a culture of continual improvement in accordance with the ISO 9001 standard under which we are certified. In addition to the objectives defined in our QHSE policy, WeShip JSC ’s employees receive appropriate training, both on board and ashore.

Striving for operational excellence in HSE

Striving for operational excellence in HSE

In line with our QHSE policy, our Quality & Safety department works to implement this policy in our fleet of vessels. By stressing three key aspects – people, processes, and tools – they strive to enable our crews to bolster the culture of safety which thrives at WeShip JSC. This culture involves closely monitoring projects from the planning stages all the way to successful completion. Because of the delicate nature of most heavy lift cargoes, they perform risk assessments, hold toolbox talks and ensure the maintenance of critical equipment to optimize safety during loading, sea passage and discharge.

HSE training and awareness

HSE training and awareness

To keep our shipboard teams fully informed about the latest HSE practices, WeShip JSC runs a comprehensive training program involving regular seminars in Germany and the Philippines. Our crews have the opportunity to provide feedback, both on aspects where we could all improve and on improvements which have already been successfully introduced. Members of our Quality & Safety department frequently board the vessels to carry out and monitor operations and training activities and participate in on-board safety committees.
