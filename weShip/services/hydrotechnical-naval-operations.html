Hydrotechnical Naval Operations

Operating in the marine environment presents risks and challenges not encountered during conventional construction.

  • WeShip leverage our in-house skills and expertise, quality plant and equipment and strategic partnerships to provide technically sound and innovative marine construction solutions that minimise these risks.
  • With proven design and construct capacity and a vast portfolio of successfully delivered projects we are the ideal partner to involve from project inception through to delivery.

Our demonstrated capabilities include:

  • Design and construction
  • Bulk cargo load out facilities
  • Commercial and recreational jetties and wharves
  • Fender systems
  • Breakwaters and rock walls
  • Asset management and maintenance

We own and operate a versatile fleet of floating plant and equipment maintained to the highest standard, and with in-house engineering, mechanical and fabrication capacities we easily adapt to meet changing project requirements.
